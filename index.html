About Passd Down

Passd Down is a student-led initiative dedicated to giving wearable clothes a second life. We collect gently used clothing from individuals and families and redistribute them to communities that can benefit from them.

♻

Reduce Waste

Keep usable garments in circulation and out of landfills.

♡

Support Communities

Help quality clothing reach people who can use it right now.

✓

Promote Sustainability

Make donation feel simple, modern, and meaningful.

Why We Started

Clothes should not lose their value just because they leave a closet.

Millions of wearable clothes end up in landfills every year.
Many families struggle to access quality clothing for everyday life.

FAQ

Quick answers before you donate.

Students, families, neighbors, and community members can donate gently used clothing.

We accept clean, wearable items like shirts, jeans, jackets, hoodies, uniforms, blankets, shoes, and bags.

Use the donation form and the team will coordinate collection details with you.

Donations are sorted, quality checked, and redistributed to people and communities that can benefit from them.
